The Mind and the Machine
How I Built Matyos to Think in Mathematics
Ahmed Hafdi  ·  PolyfdoR
proof-assistant mathematics language design MatyOS logic

A first-person account of building MatyOS — a proof-assistant language where programs are proofs and types are theorems. Hafdi traces the journey from classical logic to a three-valued truth system, and the engineering decisions that shaped the El interpreter. Part memoir, part technical manifesto: how one mind taught a machine to reason.
