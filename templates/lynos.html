The Problem

Static images fail Moroccan artisanat

Morocco is home to centuries-old artisanal traditions — intricately designed ceramics, handwoven carpets, masterful metalwork, and woodcarving. Yet on e-commerce platforms, these masterpieces are reduced to flat photographs that fail to convey depth, texture, and cultural essence.

Artisans struggle to showcase the true value of their work. Customers miss the immersive experience of exploring Moroccan craftsmanship. Traditional knowledge, passed down through generations, faces extinction as master artisans age and younger generations migrate to cities.

"What if every artisan could upload a few photos and instantly generate a professional 3D model of their product?"

— Ahmed HAFDI
The Solution

LynOS: AI-Powered 3D Reconstruction

LynOS — an intelligent system that's easy to remember, easy to use, and designed to empower artisans. Built by Ahmed HAFDI through countless hours of experimentation, coding, and refinement.

Security & IP Protection

The .lynoX File Format

The proprietary .lynoX extension represents a revolutionary approach to intellectual property protection in the digital heritage space. Unlike standard 3D formats that can be easily copied or modified, .lynoX files incorporate multiple security layers.

kas_tine.lynoX
encryption: AES-256, viewer-locked
artifact: "كاس طين" (Kas Tine — clay cup)
artisan: <attributed>
region: Morocco
technique: Hand-thrown, kiln-fired earthenware
dimensions: { height: 9.2cm, diameter: 7.8cm }
scripts: [ "ar", "ber" ]
license: educational + commercial (authorized)
drm: enabled

Vision

The Future of Moroccan Artisanat

A global virtual marketplace for Moroccan artisanat. AR-enabled cultural experiences that allow people worldwide to walk through Moroccan souks from their living rooms. A new era where artisans are empowered to showcase their craft globally — not limited by traditional marketing.

While LynOS begins with Moroccan artisanat, the platform's architecture allows expansion to other cultural traditions. The combination of AI-powered generation, secure file formats, and cultural-context preservation creates a replicable model for heritage preservation worldwide.

LynOS demonstrates that the most powerful applications of AI aren't about replacing human capabilities, but about amplifying and preserving the irreplaceable value of human cultural expression.
